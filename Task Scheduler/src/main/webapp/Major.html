Customer Service

Welcome to our website!!!


1. Click on the get started button to go to the sign in page.

2. If you have an account , then sign in directly using your username and password.

3. If you don't have an account , then click on create account. Provide your details and create your account.

4. After successful login , enter the task name and set the time for your task.

5. If you want to change your task name or timings , click on the update option and change your task details.

Note : Please enter a valid mobile number while registering , mistakes in this may lead to not receiving the notifications.

FAQ's:


1. How To Add a Task ?
   Firstly , sign up to our website and sign in with your credentials . You will be directed to a page saying Add Task . Enter your task name and time and click on the add task button to add your task.

2. How to Update a Task ?
   Go to the tasks list . You will see a button saying "update" beside the timer . Click on the update button , then you will be directed to a page where you can change the task name and task time . Click on the "update task" button to update it.
